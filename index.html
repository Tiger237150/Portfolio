Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our AI chatbot solutions.

What platform are your bots built on?

Our AI chatbots are built on a proprietary platform that combines the power of leading AI models with our custom training algorithms. This allows us to create highly personalized and effective solutions tailored to your specific business needs.

Can I use the bot on my website or WhatsApp?

Yes! Our chatbots are designed to be platform-agnostic and can be integrated with your website, WhatsApp Business API, Facebook Messenger, and other popular communication channels. We provide seamless integration support for all major platforms.

Do you offer support after delivery?

Absolutely! We provide ongoing support for 30 to 90 days after delivery, depending on your chosen package. Additionally, we offer optional maintenance plans for clients who want extended support and regular updates to their chatbot's knowledge base.

How long does bot setup take?

The typical implementation timeline is 2-3 weeks, including training, customization, and integration. Basic chatbots can be deployed faster, while more complex solutions with extensive training may take a bit longer. We'll provide you with a specific timeline during our initial consultation.

Can the chatbot integrate with my CRM system?

Yes, our chatbots can integrate with popular CRM systems like Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho, and others. This allows for seamless lead data transfer and customer information management. Custom integrations for specific systems are available upon request.
